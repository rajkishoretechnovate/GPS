Service/Calibration/Repair for
Laser Levels and Survey Tools

We offer service/calibration/repairs of our customers laser levels and survey tools. Please note that we charge an initial inspection fee of $80 in order to accurately assess the condition of your laser and understand exactly what needs to be repaired. The inspection fee is included in the total price of your repair.

We will also notify you of any additional costs to the repair of your instrument, such as part replacement and extra labour. If you live within the Perth Metropolitan area, we offer our customers a $20 charge to organise freight.
